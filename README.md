# Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

This project presents an interactive **Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze operational performance across inventory, logistics, suppliers, and revenue distribution.

## Project Objectives

The goal of this dashboard is to provide data-driven insights into supply chain operations including:

- Revenue performance by product category
- Inventory distribution across product types
- Supplier production performance
- Logistics and transportation cost analysis
- Shipping efficiency and delivery time analysis
- Supplier quality assessment through defect rates

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard consists of three analytical sections:

### 1. Supply Chain Overview
Provides high-level KPIs and revenue insights.

- Total Revenue
- Total Products Sold
- Average Shipping Time
- Average Defect Rate
- Revenue by Product Category
- Revenue by Location

### 2. Inventory & Production
Focuses on inventory management and supplier performance.

- Inventory levels by product category
- Production volume by supplier
- Manufacturing cost analysis by SKU

### 3. Logistics & Shipping
Analyzes transportation efficiency and shipping costs.

- Shipping time by carrier
- Logistics cost by transportation mode
- Shipping cost vs delivery time analysis
- Supplier defect rate evaluation
